# Heading
Heading by # and the quantity represents the number of levels of heading

# Paragraph
black line

# Line break
double space

# Bold
two * or two _
e.g. this is a **apple** 

# Italic
only one * or _

# Bold and Italic 
three * or _

# quote
one > or >>
> this is my notes of md
>> md is xxx
